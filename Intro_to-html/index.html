Despicable Me Characters

Gru is a villian with a very cold heart. His life goal is to be the best villian ever in order to make his mom proud. His enemy, Vector, steals a pyramid which ends up being the most evil scheme. Gru comes up with a plan to steal the moon to be more evil than Vector. Other characters in the movie are Margo, Edith, and Agnes they are sisters that live in an orphanage. These three girls are treated poorly and are forced to sell cookes in order to make money for the owner of the orphanage, Miss Hattie.

Plot

As Margo, Edith, and Agnes are selling cookies when they come upon Gru's house. Gru tries to get the girls to leave, but the girls are very persistant. Eventually Gru realizes he could use these girls for his evil plan to steal the moon. Gru fosters the girls while he is working to make robot cookies to spy on Vector. His plan with the girls works and he successfully steal the moon. However, through this process he grew a relationship with the girls and they got in the way of his plan. In the end, Gru returns the moon, adopts and cares for the girls, and decides to no longer be evil.
