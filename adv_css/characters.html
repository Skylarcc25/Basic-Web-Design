Harry Potter: Characters

Harry James Potter

Portrayed by: Daniel Radcliffe

Harry James Potter is the protagonist of the series, a young wizard marked as the "Chosen One" to defeat the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort. He has messy black hair, bright green eyes, and a lightning-shaped scar on his forehead, which he got as a baby when Voldemort tried to kill him. Harry is known for his bravery, loyalty, and a strong sense of justice. Raised by his cruel aunt and uncle, he discovers his magical heritage at the age of 11 and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Hermione Granger

Portrayed by: Emma Watson

Hermione Jean Granger is one of Harry’s best friends and one of the brightest witches of her age. Known for her intelligence, resourcefulness, and strong sense of right and wrong, Hermione is fiercely loyal and always seeks to solve problems with knowledge. With bushy brown hair, brown eyes, and often seen with a book in hand, Hermione plays a crucial role in the trio’s adventures and is a key figure in the fight against Voldemort.
